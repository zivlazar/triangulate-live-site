Privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you play Triangulate or visit this website.

1. Information we collect

We collect information necessary to provide the Triangulate game experience:

  • Location data: Real-time GPS coordinates are used to determine your position on the map during active gameplay. Location is only accessed while the app is in use and with your explicit permission.
  • Player profile: A randomly generated player ID and an optional nickname you choose.
  • Gameplay data: Scores, triangle captures, match history, and leaderboard rankings.
  • Device information: Device type and operating system version for compatibility and performance optimisation.
  • Analytics events: In-game events and consent-based website analytics used to help us improve the game and site. We do not use your phone number as an automatic analytics identifier.

2. How we use your information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Power the real-time multiplayer map and triangle mechanics.
  • Display leaderboards and maintain fair rankings.
  • Monitor and improve game performance and stability.
  • Run product experiments and A/B tests to improve the user experience.
  • Detect and prevent cheating, abuse, or misuse of the service.

3. Location data

Triangulate is a location-based game that requires GPS access to function. We do not store your precise location history beyond the current active session. Location data is transmitted securely and is never sold or shared with third-party advertisers.

You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings, though doing so will prevent you from playing the game.

4. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Secure backend database for leaderboard data and player profiles.
  • Mixpanel: Analytics for anonymised gameplay and product events. You can opt out at mixpanel.com/optout.
  • MapLibre / Protomaps: Vector maps are loaded on demand and cached locally. No personal profile data is intentionally shared with map providers.

5. Data retention

Leaderboard and gameplay data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time via our contact form. We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Children's privacy

Triangulate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to safeguard your data against unauthorised access or disclosure.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data.
  • Request your data in a structured format where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, use our contact form.

9. Website-specific notes

This website is a marketing site for Triangulate. If you browse the site without contacting us, we only use analytics when you explicitly consent. The leaderboard pages may request public gameplay data from the Triangulate backend so the site can display recent scores and activity in read-only form.

If you use the contact form, we store the details you submit so we can respond, keep a server-side record of website feedback, and send a confirmation email back to you. If you opt in to analytics on the form, we may also associate the enquiry with an existing Triangulate player ID already present on the same device. We do not use your phone number itself as the cross-context identifier.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, use our contact form.

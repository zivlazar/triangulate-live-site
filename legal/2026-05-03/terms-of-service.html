1. What Triangulate is

Triangulate is a team-based map game. Each team has three player slots. A slot can be filled by an At home player, an Outside player, or an AI player.

At home players move by tapping the map. Outside players move using live device location. AI players are computer-controlled players provided by Triangulate.

The main way to start a game is Play Triangulate. The lobby lets players build a team with Invite friends, Find teammate, Add AI, and Enemy: Local | Country | Global | AI.

2. Children and parents

Outside play uses live location and may involve real-world movement. Children should be supervised appropriately when playing Outside.

3. Playing At home

At home players move by tapping the map. At home movement may be route-limited, speed-limited, and path-constrained. At home players must not automate movement, exploit bugs, teleport freely, or control another player's marker.

4. Playing Outside

Outside players move using live device location.

To play Outside, you must:

6. Teams and arenas

A match uses one shared arena. If one or more friendly players choose Outside, the arena is based on the real-world area of the Outside player or players.

If multiple Outside players are too far apart to share one arena, the lobby may block match start and suggest that one player switches to At home.

If all friendly players are At home or AI, the match uses the host's selected area. Remote players and remote enemies may take part in that arena as At home players.

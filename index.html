How It Works

Simple rules, deep strategy — played out across your real neighbourhood.

1
👥

Form a Team

Join or create a lobby with two teammates. Each player becomes a vertex of your triangle.

2
📍

Move on the Map

Walk, run, or cycle around your city. Your real GPS position updates live on the shared map.

3
△

Form Triangles

Your team's three positions automatically form a triangle. The bigger the area, the more territory you own.

4
⚡

Cut & Score

When your triangle's edge crosses through a rival triangle, it scores a cut — shrink them to zero to eliminate.
